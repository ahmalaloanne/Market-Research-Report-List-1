<p><p>The Threaded Tees market includes various types of fittings used in piping systems. **Threaded Fitting Tees** are general-purpose connectors with threaded ends for joining pipes. **Threaded Equal Tees** have all three outlets of the same size, ensuring uniform flow distribution. **Threaded Unequal Tees** feature one outlet of a different size, allowing for flow redirection or branching into pipes of varying diameters. These tees are widely used in plumbing, oil & gas, and industrial applications for secure, leak-proof connections.</p></p>
