<p><p>The Terahertz sensors market is divided into two main types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing utilizes light-based techniques, such as lasers and photonic crystals, to detect terahertz waves, offering high sensitivity and resolution for various applications, including spectroscopy and imaging. In contrast, electronic sensing employs electronic circuits and devices to measure terahertz frequencies, providing robust performance in industrial and telecommunications sectors. Both types play a crucial role in advancing technologies in security, medical diagnostics, and material characterization.</p></p>
