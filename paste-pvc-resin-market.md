<p><p>Paste PVC resin is used in applications requiring a smooth, flexible finish, such as coatings, flooring, and synthetic leather. The market is divided into homogeneous and heterogeneous types. Homogeneous paste PVC resin has a uniform molecular structure, offering consistent properties like high transparency and stability, ideal for high-quality applications. Heterogeneous paste PVC resin has a varied molecular structure, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness, suitable for less demanding uses. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing performance and affordability.</p></p>
