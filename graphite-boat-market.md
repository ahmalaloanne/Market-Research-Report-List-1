<p><ul><li>Monocrystal Graphite Boat</li><li>Polycrystal Graphite Boat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphite boat market is categorized into two main types: monocrystal graphite boats and polycrystal graphite boats. Monocrystal graphite boats are made from a single crystal structure, offering superior thermal and electrical conductivity, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as semiconductor manufacturing. On the other hand, polycrystal graphite boats consist of multiple crystal structures, providing good thermal stability and cost-effectiveness, suitable for various industries including electronics and solar energy. Both types serve essential roles in thermal management and material deposition processes.</p></p>
