<p><p>The nonfat milk powder market is categorized into three main types based on processing treatments: low-heat, medium-heat, and high-heat processing. Low-heat processing preserves more nutrients and is ideal for products requiring higher nutritional quality, while medium-heat processing enhances solubility and is often used in food applications. High-heat processing, which involves higher temperatures, is commonly used for products that need longer shelf life and are less concerned with nutrient retention, making it suitable for baked goods and confectioneries.</p></p>
