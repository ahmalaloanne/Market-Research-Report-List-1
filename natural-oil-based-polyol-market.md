<p><strong>The Natural Oil-Based Polyol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyether Polyols</li><li>Polyester Polyols</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural oil-based polyol market is primarily divided into two types: polyether polyols and polyester polyols. Polyether polyols are derived from renewable sources and are known for their flexibility and resistance to hydrolysis, making them ideal for applications like flexible foams and adhesives. In contrast, polyester polyols are synthesized from natural oils and are valued for their superior mechanical properties and thermal stability, making them suitable for rigid foams, coatings, and elastomers. Both types contribute to sustainable formulations in various industries.</p></p>
