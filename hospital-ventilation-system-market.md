<p><strong>The Hospital Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation Systems</li><li>Supply Ventilation Systems</li><li>Balanced Ventilation Systems</li><li>Energy Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hospital ventilation system market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Exhaust Ventilation Systems** remove stale air, ensuring a constant supply of fresh air, crucial for infection control.</p><p>2. **Supply Ventilation Systems** introduce conditioned fresh air into spaces, maintaining air quality and thermal comfort.</p><p>3. **Balanced Ventilation Systems** combine both supply and exhaust methods, providing optimal air quality and energy efficiency.</p><p>4. **Energy Recovery Systems** reclaim energy from exhaust air to pre-condition incoming fresh air, enhancing overall energy efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
