<p><p>The Intelligent Braking System (IBS) is an advanced automotive safety technology designed to enhance vehicle braking efficiency and safety. <p><ul><li>Two-Box System</li><li>One-Box System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent Braking System (IBS) market includes Two-Box and One-Box systems. The Two-Box System separates the brake control unit from the hydraulic unit, offering flexibility and modularity, often used in traditional and hybrid vehicles. The One-Box System integrates both units into a single compact module, enhancing efficiency, reducing weight, and improving response time, making it ideal for electric and advanced autonomous vehicles. Both systems aim to optimize braking performance, safety, and energy recovery, catering to evolving automotive technologies.</p></p>
