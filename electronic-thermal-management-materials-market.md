<p><p>The Electronic Thermal Management Materials market includes various types designed to manage heat in electronic devices. Conductive pastes are used for bonding and heat dissipation. Conductive tapes provide adhesive solutions with thermal conductivity. Phase Change Materials (PCMs) absorb and release heat during phase transitions. Gap fillers fill air gaps to enhance heat transfer. Thermal greases are applied between surfaces to improve thermal conductivity. These materials ensure efficient heat dissipation, preventing overheating and enhancing the performance and lifespan of electronic components.</p></p>
