<p><p>The accelerometer and vibration sensor market is segmented into AC-Type and DC-Type sensors. AC-Type sensors are designed to measure dynamic vibrations and are typically used in applications where sensing changes in motion or oscillation is crucial, such as in automotive and aerospace industries. DC-Type sensors, on the other hand, measure static and low-frequency accelerations, making them ideal for applications like structural health monitoring and inclination measurement. Both types serve distinct purposes depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
