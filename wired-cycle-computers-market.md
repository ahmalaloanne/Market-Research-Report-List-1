<p><p>Wired cycle computers can be categorized into two main market types: analog and smart. They are usually more affordable and straightforward, appealing to traditional cyclists. In contrast, smart cycle computers integrate advanced technology, including GPS, Bluetooth connectivity, and multifunctional apps.
