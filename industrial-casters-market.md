<p><p>The industrial casters market comprises two primary types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature a wheel that can rotate 360 degrees, allowing for enhanced maneuverability in tight spaces and versatility in movement direction. They are ideal for applications requiring frequent directional changes. In contrast, rigid casters have fixed wheels, providing straight-line mobility and stability, making them suitable for transporting heavy loads over long distances. Both types are essential for optimizing efficiency in various industrial settings.</p></p>
